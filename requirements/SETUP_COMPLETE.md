## 💡 Tips for Best Experience

### For Voice Commands:

1. **Wait for prompt** - Speak after "Listening..."
2. **Speak clearly** - Normal pace, not too fast
3. **Reduce noise** - Quiet environment works best
4. **Good mic** - Headset is better than built-in
5. **Distance** - 6-12 inches from mic

### For Better Responses:

1. **Be specific** - "open chrome" not "open browser"
2. **Natural language** - Ollama understands context
3. **Ask questions** - It can answer with Ollama
4. **Use memory** - It remembers your preferences
