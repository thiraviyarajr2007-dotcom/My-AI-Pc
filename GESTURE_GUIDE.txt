┌────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│  HOW TO TEST GESTURES                                      │
└────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘

  1. Double-click: TEST_GESTURES.bat
  2. Camera window will open
  3. Show your hand to the camera
  4. Make a gesture
  5. Hold steady for 2-3 seconds
  6. Watch the action execute!
  7. Press ESC to quit

┌────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│  GESTURE REFERENCE                                         │
└────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘

  1 FINGER (Point):
  ─────────────────
       ☝️
      👆
     Index finger up, others down
     → Opens Chrome

  2 FINGERS (Peace):
  ──────────────────
       ✌️
      ✌️
     Index + Middle fingers up
     → Takes Screenshot

  3 FINGERS:
  ──────────
       🤟
      👌
     Index + Middle + Ring up
     → Opens Notepad

  4 FINGERS:
  ──────────
       🖐️
      ✋
     All except thumb
     → Opens Calculator

  5 FINGERS (Open Palm):
  ──────────────────────
       🖐️
      ✋
     All fingers extended
     → Opens File Explorer

┌────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│  TIPS FOR BEST RESULTS                                     │
└────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘

  ✓ Good Lighting
    • Face a window or light source
    • Avoid backlighting
    • Ensure hand is well-lit

  ✓ Clear Background
    • Plain wall works best
    • Avoid cluttered backgrounds
    • Keep hand in focus

  ✓ Steady Hand
    • Hold gesture for 2-3 seconds
    • Don't move too much
    • Keep hand in camera view

  ✓ Proper Distance
    • 1-2 feet from camera
    • Show full hand
    • Not too close or far

  ✓ Clear Gestures
    • Make distinct finger positions
    • Fully extend or fold fingers
    • Avoid in-between positions

┌────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│  TROUBLESHOOTING                                           │
└────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘

  Hand Not Detected:
  ─────────────────
  • Check camera is working
  • Improve lighting
  • Move hand closer
  • Show full hand to camera

  Wrong Finger Count:
  ──────────────────
  • Make clearer gestures
  • Fully extend fingers
  • Keep hand steady
  • Check lighting

  Action Not Executing:
  ────────────────────
  • Hold gesture longer (2-3 seconds)
  • Watch progress bar fill up
  • Keep hand steady
  • Wait for cooldown (3 seconds between actions)

  Camera Not Opening:
  ──────────────────
  • Check webcam is connected
  • Close other apps using camera
  • Restart computer
  • Check camera permissions
